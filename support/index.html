FAQ · 01

How do I create a squad?

After signing up and creating your club, head to the My Squad tab. You'll have a £920m wage cap to spend across 15 players — 11 in your starting XI, 3 subs, and 1 reserve. Use the Scout Report to filter and pick players, then save your squad before the next gameweek deadline.

FAQ · 02

How does scoring work?

Players earn points across 24 stat categories every gameweek — goals, assists, shots on target, key passes, tackles, blocks, interceptions, saves, clean sheets, and more. Your weekly points are the sum of your starting XI's totals, with a ×2 multiplier for your captain and ×1.5 for your vice-captain. Positions are weighted differently — for example, a goalkeeper clean sheet is worth more than a forward's.

FAQ · 03

What is the Dugout?

The Dugout is the in-league group chat for every league you're part of. Talk smack, react to live scores, and coordinate with the rest of your league in real time. It's also where you'll find league settings and member tools.

FAQ · 04

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account and associated data from inside the app, or by emailing us if you no longer have the app installed. Full instructions are on our Account Deletion page.

FAQ · 05

How do I report a problem or inappropriate content?

For account issues, bugs, or general support, email legal@elitescoutfootball.com. To report inappropriate messages or behaviour in a league chat, use the in-app report option in the Dugout — long-press a message to flag it for review. Our Community Guidelines set out what is and isn't allowed.
