Account Deletion

EliteScout Fantasy Football

You can delete your EliteScout Fantasy Football account and associated data at any time.

01

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open EliteScout Fantasy Football and sign in.
  2. Go to the Dugout tab and open Settings.
  3. Select Delete Account and confirm. Your account and data will be permanently deleted.
02

If you no longer have the app installed

Email support@elitescoutfootball.com from the email address linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my account." We will process your request and confirm once complete.

03

What is deleted

Your account, profile, email address, squad data, league memberships, and Dugout messages are permanently removed.

04

What may be retained

We may retain limited records where legally required (e.g. transaction records) for the period required by law, after which they are deleted. See our Privacy Policy for details.
