Community Guidelines

Last updated: 13 June 2026

These Community Guidelines govern your use of the Dugout and any other feature of EliteScout Football ("ESF", "we", "us") that lets you post messages, team names, or other content that other users can see ("User Content"). By using any of these features, you agree to follow these Guidelines.

01

You are responsible for what you post

You are solely responsible for the User Content you post. Only post content you have the right to share, and remember that other members of your league can see it.

02

Zero tolerance for objectionable content

There is no tolerance for objectionable content or abusive behaviour on ESF. You must not post, share, or transmit content that:

  • harasses, bullies, threatens, or intimidates any person;
  • is hateful or discriminatory on the basis of race, ethnicity, national origin, religion, disability, gender, age, sexual orientation, or gender identity;
  • is sexually explicit, obscene, or pornographic;
  • promotes or depicts violence, self-harm, or illegal activity;
  • is spam, advertising, a scam, or promotes gambling or betting services;
  • impersonates another person or misrepresents your affiliation with any person or organisation;
  • shares another person's private or identifying information without their consent;
  • infringes anyone's intellectual property or other rights; or
  • is otherwise unlawful or objectionable.
03

Reporting and blocking

If you see content that breaches these Guidelines, use the in-app Report option on the relevant message or user, or email us at legal@elitescoutfootball.com. You can also block any user so that you no longer see their content.

04

How we respond

We review reports of objectionable content and aim to act within 24 hours — removing the content and, where appropriate, suspending or permanently removing the user responsible. We may remove any User Content and suspend or terminate any account at our discretion, with or without notice, for any breach of these Guidelines.

05

No endorsement

User Content reflects the views of the user who posted it, not those of ESF. We do not endorse, and are not responsible for, User Content posted by users.

06

Changes to these Guidelines

We may update these Guidelines from time to time. We will indicate the date of the most recent change above. Your continued use of ESF after changes take effect means you accept the updated Guidelines.
